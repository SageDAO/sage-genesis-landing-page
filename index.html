Genesis Community Raise

Sage will offer a Genesis Open Edition NFT, which will grant access and tokens to the DAO. No prior funding has been accepted by Sage, which ensures a fair distribution to the community. The Open Edition will live for seven days before the raise comes to a close. Once the raise has concluded, the tokens will be distributed to the NFT holders proportionally to the amount of open editions they have held. 10% of the funds raised will be set aside for liquidity on uniswap.
